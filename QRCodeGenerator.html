Everything you need to know about our QR Code Generator tool

You can encode various types of data including URLs, plain text, email addresses, phone numbers, SMS messages, WiFi credentials, contact cards (vCard), and much more. Our generator supports all standard QR code data formats.

Error correction helps your QR code remain scannable even if it's partially damaged or obscured. Low (7%) works for clean environments, Medium (15%) is recommended for most uses, Quartile (25%) for environments where damage is possible, and High (30%) for maximum durability but larger code size.

No, your privacy is completely protected. All QR code generation happens locally in your browser. Your data is never sent to our servers, stored, or shared with anyone. The QR codes are generated entirely on your device.

You can download your QR codes in PNG and JPEG formats. PNG is recommended for the best quality and transparency support. You can also copy the QR code image directly to your clipboard for quick pasting into other applications.
