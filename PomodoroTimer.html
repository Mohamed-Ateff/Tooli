Everything you need to know about our Pomodoro Timer tool

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method that uses a timer to break work into intervals, typically 25 minutes of focused work followed by a 5-minute break. After four work sessions, a longer break (15-30 minutes) is taken to recharge.

Set your desired work, short break, and long break durations. Click "Start" to begin a work session. The timer will automatically switch between work and break sessions. Use "Pause" to temporarily stop and "Reset" to start over.

Yes, you can adjust the work duration (1-60 minutes), short break duration (1-30 minutes), and long break duration (1-60 minutes) to suit your needs. The default settings are 25 minutes for work, 5 minutes for short breaks, and 15 minutes for long breaks.

After completing four work sessions, the timer automatically switches to a long break to help you recharge. Once the long break is complete, the cycle restarts with a new work session.

Yes, the timer plays a gentle sound when a work or break session ends to alert you. Ensure your device's sound is enabled to hear the notification.
