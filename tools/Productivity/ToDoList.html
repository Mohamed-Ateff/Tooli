Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our To-Do List Manager

Simply type your task in the input field at the top and click "Add Task" or press Enter. Your task will be immediately added to your list with a pending status.

Yes! Click the checkbox next to any task to mark it as completed. Completed tasks will be crossed out and moved to a less prominent visual state, but remain in your list for reference.

Your tasks are stored locally in your browser, so they'll persist between sessions on the same device and browser. However, clearing your browser data will remove your tasks. For important lists, consider copying them elsewhere as backup.

Each task has a red delete button (×) on the right side. Click it to permanently remove the task from your list. You can also use "Clear Completed" to remove all completed tasks at once.

There's no hard limit on the number of tasks, but each task can be up to 200 characters long. For very large lists (100+ tasks), you might notice slower performance depending on your device.

The statistics section shows your pending tasks, completed tasks, and completion rate percentage. This helps you track your productivity and see how much progress you're making on your goals.
