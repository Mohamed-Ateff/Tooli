Everything you need to know about our Text Case Converter tool

Our Text Case Converter allows you to instantly change the case of your text to uppercase, lowercase, title case, or sentence case. It's perfect for formatting text for writing, coding, or design purposes.

Title case capitalizes the first letter of each word, ideal for headings or titles (e.g., "The Quick Brown Fox"). Sentence case capitalizes only the first letter of each sentence, suitable for standard text (e.g., "The quick brown fox. Jumps over the lazy dog.").
